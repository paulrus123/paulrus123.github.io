Autonomous Vehicle Parking

Overview

At Zenuity, I wrote the infotainment software for interacting with the vehicle's autonomous vehicle parking technology. I also contributed to the driver monitoring system, which tracks the driver's eyes and head to determine if they are looking at the road.

My team demo'd our automated valet parking system at the 2021 Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas. I was lucky enough to be in attendance and was actually the pedestrian in this video!
