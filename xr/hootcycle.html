Hootcycle

A comprehensive mobile bike computer for the cycling community

Overview

Hootcycle is a mobile bike computer app that helps cyclists track their rides, explore new routes, and keep their battery from dying. I co-founded the project and built the entire app using React Native, integrating native modules to ensure a responsive, cross-platform and battery optimized experience. My co-founder led the design.
